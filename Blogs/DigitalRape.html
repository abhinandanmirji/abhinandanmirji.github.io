Introduction
Digital rape does not refer to the sexual offence commited online or anyother activity online. There is a common understanding of people that it refers to cyber crime or sexual activity commited online. If any person insertes finger, thumb and toe into another person's body, the act is called Digital Rape.

This was not an offence, until 2012 December it came under the ambit of molestation. It was considered as rape only after Nirbhaya Rape, after which the parliament made an amendment which called it a rape.
To whom it doesn't apply?
It applies to bothe minor and major. The digital rape in India was rapidly increasing hence, the punishment for it has been rigorous.
In India, the scope of it was not widned but it is now considered as an offence under section 375 of IPC and some other provisions of IPC. It is also considered as an offence under Section 3 and few more sections under POSCO act.
Punishment for Digital Rape
Usually under POSCO act it is for 5 years but under IPC it varies from 10 years to life imprisionment.
Proper meaning of Diglital Rape
A person inserting any of his fingers, toe or object into a women's vagina, urethra, mouth or anus without her consent is called as digital rape.
Conlusion
In the recent times it has been considered as rape and there is strict punishment for it under the Indian laws. Criminal Appeal Number 7332/2018- Mahendra Singh Gonda v. State of Madhya Pradesh.
In the above mentioned case, the Madhya Pradesh High Court upheld capital punishment given to convict for digital rape of a 4 and half year old girl.
